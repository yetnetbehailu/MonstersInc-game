Instructions

In a world inhabited by monsters, the city of Monstropolis is powered by energy from the screams of human children. At the Monsters, Inc. factory, skilled monsters employed as "scarers" venture into the human world to scare children and harvest their screams, through doors that activate portals to children's bedroom closets. The monsters are counting on you to help them with their mission!

- You need to find all the matching card pairs before time is up!
If the two cards turned picture-side-up are identical then it's a match. For each match made you will receive 10 points. Depending on the game level a time bonus will be added when a pair is found.

Game Level Time Bonus
Easy 0seconds
Medium 3seconds
Hard 3seconds

- However be careful not to make too many mistakes as your score will be deducted 2 points for each mismatch(note no deduction below 0).

- Depending on game level there will be a draw limit.

Game Level Flip limit Pair found
Easy No limit No bonus
Medium 5 Flips +1 each pair found
Hard 10 Flips +1 each pair found

- Press to re-load game or to get back to home page.

- If you would like to listen to some groovy music before game starts click on click again to turn it off.
